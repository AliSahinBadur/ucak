PRIVACY

The DefensX browser extension for Mozilla requires your permission to store and maintain information about the web addresses (URLs) you visit. We also collect limited aggregated statistical data about how you use the DefensX extension. The information we collect and maintain is used to help us improve and optimize DefensX so that we can continue to provide you with the best possible secure browsing experience.

Can we collect the following data that personally identifies you:
- Collect and store websites you visit
- Websites from which you have uploaded or downloaded files
- Websites where you have used a password that was previously exposed in a data breach
Without this permission, you will not able to use the DefensX extension.
Can we collect the following anonymous data about your use of DefensX Mozilla Extension:
- Collecting data on blocked trackers and ads to help improve our adblocker engine
For details, see our privacy policy
